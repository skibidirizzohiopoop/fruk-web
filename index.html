WHAT IS FRUK?

FrUk is the ship between France and England - countries from the series 'Hetalia: Axis Powers.' Both of these countries are apart of the 'Allied Forces' from World War II. FrUk is one of the most popular ships in the fandom - FrUk shippers rivaling UsUk (America x England) shippers. Both of these countries grew up together as close frenemies since the Middle Ages. These two nations debuted in the first episode of season 1 during the World Conference Meeting, and are later on well known as rivals.
